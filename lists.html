  2. andrapradesh
    • Tirupati
      • Tirupati is a city in Andhra Pradesh, India, famous for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, a major pilgrimage site. It's a spiritual and cultural hub with hills and forests nearby 🌳.
    • vijayavad
      • Vijayawada is a city in Andhra Pradesh, India, situated on the Krishna River. It's known for its temples, like Kanaka Durga Temple, and is a hub for commerce and education. The city's scenic surroundings include the Prakasam Barrage
    • Guntur
      • Guntur is a city in Andhra Pradesh, India, known for its agricultural markets, especially chilies and tobacco. It's also a hub for education and healthcare, with nearby attractions like Amaravati and the Krishna River 🌳.
    • Kurnool
      • Kurnool is a city in Andhra Pradesh, India, with a rich history of Buddhist and Hindu heritage. It's known for its textiles, agriculture, and being a transit point for nearby attractions like the Srisailam wildlife sanctuary and temple 🏞️.
    • Visakhapatnam
      • Visakhapatnam, or Vizag, is a major port city in Andhra Pradesh, India, blending industry with natural beauty. It's known for its scenic coastline, beaches like Ramakrishna Beach, and hills like Kailash Giri 🌊.
  3. tamilanadu
    • Chennai
      • Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant coastal city blending tradition and modernity 🌊. Known for its beaches (Marina), cultural heritage (Kapaleeswarar Temple), and industries (automotive, IT), it's a hub for arts, food, and commerce in South India
    • Coimbatore
      • Coimbatore, in Tamil Nadu, is a bustling industrial and textile hub 🌊. Known as the 'Manchester of South India', it's famous for engineering, textiles, and its proximity to the Nilgiri Hills, making it a gateway to Western Ghats 🌳.
    • Madurai
      • Madurai, in Tamil Nadu, is an ancient city known for the iconic Meenakshi Amman Temple 🎉. It's a cultural hub with a rich history, vibrant festivals, and traditional arts, showcasing the essence of Tamil heritage.
    • Trichy (Tiruchirappalli)
      • Trichy (Tiruchirappalli) is a city in Tamil Nadu, known for its rockfort temple and spiritual landmarks 🌟. It's a blend of history, culture, and commerce, with attractions like the Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple and Ucchi Pillayar Temple.
    • Salem
      • Salem is a city in Tamil Nadu, known for its steel production and mango cultivation 🌳. It's surrounded by hills (Shevaroy Hills, Kadhambur Hills) and has historical sites like the Salem Fort, blending industry with natural charm.
